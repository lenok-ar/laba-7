Vot i prishla myagkaya, zolotistaya i takaya nepredskazuemaya osen'. Zheltye list'ya sletayut s vetok derev'yev, pokryvaya zemlyu shurshashchim odeyalom. Pod ogromnym starym dubom razbrosany spelye zheludy. Nedaleko slyshen shorok upavshey vetki i tresk osiny. Reshetochatyy zabor vozle pokosivshenosya doma stoit v sykhikh zaroslyakh travy. Zheskiy veter pribyl k nemu goru opavshey list'vy. Kazhetsya, chto zabor obnesen sugrobami, tol'ko zhelto-oranzhevymi. Tikhiy dozhdik, slovno posledniy shepot oseni, melko zamorosil po krisham. Nebo stalo tyazheloye i nizkoye. Osen', smeniv leto, stala polnopravno khozyaynichat' vsyudu.
